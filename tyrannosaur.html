Tyrannosaurus Rex
(Tyrant Lizard King)

Bio: The Tyrannosaurus Rex was the dominant predator of the Cretaceous Period. It strong body and powerful jaw meant it was perfectly adapted to catch prey.
Period: Creataceous
Size: Approx. 40 ft long and 12 ft tall
Weight: Somewhere between 11,000 and 15,000 pounds
Diet Type: Carnivore
Habitat: Western North America, including Montana and South Dakota
